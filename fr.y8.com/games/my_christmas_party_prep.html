Christmas is one of the most anticipated holidays. Since childhood, Christmas has a special meaning in the life of every person. That's the case with this lovely girl. She starts her preparation by selecting a representative Christmas outfit, to which she will match an elegant make-up and which will highlight her daring features. In the following levels you will have the opportunity to cook the tastiest Christmas cookies. Her crush will arrive at any moment to help her decorate the tree.
