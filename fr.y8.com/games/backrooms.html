Backrooms is a horror game with all lights on, based on a Creepypasta lore of the same name. You found yourself trapped in a strange dimension with endless number of corridors, with lights on full blast, no furniture in sight, and a looming presence that is out of sight. Explore each corridor and try to find your way to the exit. Can you exit before the time runs out? Enjoy playing this game here at Y8.com!
