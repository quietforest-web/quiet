You are a mischievous ghost, and your only joy is to sneak behind people and scare them. You lose your ability if they spot you and may vanish if stay in their “vision” cone. Use your tactical skills to hunt them down. Collect the skulls to gain extra points and hunt humans to complete the levels. As initially levels will be easy, but the difficulty will increase as the level increases. Be in stealth mode and have fun while hunting the humans. Play a lot more ghost games only on y8.com
