Détails concernant le jeu

Santa Haircut a trendy stylish game for our lovely Santa this Christmas season. Our Santa wants to reach out to all the kids for present gifts this Christmas season. As we know he will be very cute and also will be having a specific style, for a change now we will be his style manager and let's style him for this season, and let's celebrate our favorite Christmas season with new looking Santa. We have to help him with the styling like dressing his hair by washing, ironing cutting, and trimming. As his hair and beard are very much grown, select and make the latest fashioned hair. Next, let's select the dress for our cute Santa according to the hairstyle. Finally let us select the gift and help Santa to look elegant, handsome, or cute. Play this Santa game this Christmas season only on y8.com
