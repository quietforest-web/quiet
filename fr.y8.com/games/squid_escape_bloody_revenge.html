Détails concernant le jeu

You are trapped in the Prison. But not a regular one. This one is on Abandoned Island and its full of traps. Prisoners and Guards are here to kill you. To survive you must fight, kill and solve some puzzles. It's time to survive in this game Squid Escape: Bloody Revenge tries to escape from these games full of action and blood! Control your character and take him to the exit of this island but be careful that the soldiers are very attentive and will not hesitate to eliminate you. There are a lot of deadly games here and you must follow the direction to pass the level. Enjoy playing this game here at Y8.com!
