Détails concernant le jeu

Remember the classic snake game we used to play on our mobile phones before? Well, Santa Snake gives you that kind of game play but this game certainly has its own twist. Play with other players across the globe in this exciting multiplayer snake game. Choose your favorite Christmas-themed character and compete with others. The only rule to this game is in order for you to grow, you should collect all the orbs available along the way or better yet, let other players bump you and collect the orbs from them. Have fun playing this awesome game!
