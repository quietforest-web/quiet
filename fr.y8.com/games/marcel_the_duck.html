Détails concernant le jeu

Marcel the Duck is a visual novel game where you play as Marcel the pre-teen pre-teen duck stuck on a retirement island for a whole week during summer break. His dad, Oliver, asks him to help with the management of the place. You'll have to run some errands, deliver the mail to the residents and get to know your neighbors. Help him run errands for his dad and get to meet and know other people in the island. Have fun playing this game here at Y8.com!
